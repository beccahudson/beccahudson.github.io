I am a freelance Shopify and Wordpress developer; working directly with designers to bring their clients vision to life.

I work remotely from my residence just outside of Orlando, FL. where I was born and raised.

I am an insomniac and code best at night, running off a coffee IV drip; while listening to music and getting sucked into a time warp.

I'm an introverted extrovert and need a nap after a party of more than 5. I am a nerd and I raise my kids as nerds and I'm proud of it.

I love creating something from nothing, especially if it involves a puzzle. I have mild OCD and don't always know when to quit.

I started coding 5 years ago and haven't stopped. I am a life learner and I don't feel fulfilled unless there's more to know.
